How It Works

Learn the basics of making predictions and earning points

{# Tutorial Steps #}
{# Step 1 #}
1

Make Your Predictions

Answer questions about the NBA season before it starts. Predict things like:

  • MVP and major award winners
  • Player statistics (points, rebounds, assists)
  • Team standings and playoff results
  • Season matchups and championships

Tip: Visit the Submissions page to view and make your predictions.

{# Step 2 #}
2

Earn Points Throughout the Season

As the NBA season progresses, your predictions are graded automatically:

  • Correct predictions earn you points
  • Closer guesses on stats earn partial credit
  • Points accumulate over the entire season

Tip: Each question has a point value - higher stakes for tougher predictions!

{# Step 3 #}
3

Compete on the Leaderboard

Track your progress and compete with other players:

  • See your rank and total points
  • Compare your predictions with others
  • Climb to the top to prove you're the best NBA predictor

Tip: Check the Leaderboard to see how you stack up!

{# Important Notes #}

Important Things to Know

  • Predictions must be made before the season starts or before each game
  • Once submitted, you cannot change your predictions
  • Points are awarded automatically as season results are finalized
  • Check your submissions page to see which predictions are still open
